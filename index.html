Crystals Collector

You will be given a random number.

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on the crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
